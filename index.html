Vision

Redefining public leadership

Mission

Nurture principled leaders with moral courage and imagination, to work on strengthening four pillars of Indian democracy- legislative, executive, judiciary and media

Goals and Milestones

By 2047, 25% elected representatives in Parliament and State assemblies of India, will be principled leaders working collaboratively to make democracy work for all citizens.
